What are the symptoms of a faulty battery management system?
According to the Tesla Model 3 repair manual, symptoms of a faulty battery management system include: 1. Warning lights on dashboard 2. Reduced driving range 3. Slow charging 4. Battery temperature warnings 5. Vehicle won't start
📖 Source: Tesla_Model3.pdf - Section 4.2 Battery Management System
How do I diagnose this issue?
Follow these diagnostic steps: 1. Connect diagnostic scanner 2. Check error codes 3. Test battery voltage 4. Inspect connections 5. Review system logs
📖 Source: Tesla_Model3.pdf - Section 5.1 Diagnostic Procedures

✨ Key Features Demonstrated

💬 Intelligent Chat

Ask questions in natural language and get instant answers from repair manuals with citations showing the source.

🎤 Voice Input

Use the microphone button to record your question. The system transcribes it automatically using Groq API.

📤 Manual Upload

Drag and drop PDF repair manuals. The system automatically processes and indexes them for searching.

📋 Chat History

All conversations are saved automatically. You can rename, review, and delete conversations anytime.

🌙 Dark Mode

Toggle between light and dark themes for comfortable use in different lighting conditions.

📚 Multi-Manual Support

Upload and manage multiple repair manuals. The system searches across all of them simultaneously.
